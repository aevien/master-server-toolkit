***Client***
THIS EXAMPLE DEMONSTRATES CLIENT AUTH PROCESS AND PROFILES

1. Click Play button
2. If <color=yellow>"ConnectOnStart"</color> checkbox on <color=yellow>"--CONNECTION_TO_MASTER"</color> is checked on then client will try to automatically connect to master server
3. After successful connection you will see the Sign In form. If you have an account just input your username and password and click "Submit" button
4. If you have no account yet try to sign up. Click "I have no account" button and input your credentials. Click "Submit "button.
5. After successful account creation the SignIn form will be opened. See section 4
6. If you account email is not confirmed yet you are required to do it right after signed in. Click "Get Confirmation Code" button and code will be sent to email you have signed up with. You also are required to setup smtp mailer settings. You cane find it among modules list.
7. After you signed in your profile will be loaded and its updates will be received each 5 seconds

PS: If you wish to test builds click "Tools/MSF/Build/Demos/Basic Profiles" and build your standalone version.
